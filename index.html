About Me

My name is Nikki Reed. I’m an MA Public History student at Northeastern University. I received my BA in Art History and English at Grand Valley State University. My interests are in Digital Archiving, Digital Curating, and Collections Management. In addition I have a background in Art Education and Fine Arts.

I curate an Art History blog, Women In Art History, that showcases women artists and subjects in art history.

You can find links to my CV, and a gallery of my artwork, family day and student, and my online exhibition design.
